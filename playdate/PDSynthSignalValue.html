    Alias PDSynthSignalValue

    A PDSynthSignalValue represents a signal that can be used as an input to a modulator. Its PDSynthSignal subclass is used for "active" signals that change their values automatically. PDSynthLFO and PDSynthEnvelope are subclasses of PDSynthSignal.
